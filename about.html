About Knoxville Chiropractic Care Reader

An independent local-information site about chiropractic care in Knoxville, Tennessee, with a focus on Bell Family Chiropractic in West Knoxville.

What This Site Is

This site exists to help Knoxville-area readers understand what chiropractic care actually involves — services, conditions, what to expect over time — before they pick up the phone. The information here is general and educational; it isn't a substitute for an in-person evaluation. For booking, billing, insurance verification, or anything specific to a patient's case, contact the practice directly.

About the Practice We Cover

Bell Family Chiropractic is a family-oriented chiropractic office in West Knoxville offering chiropractic adjustments, spinal decompression, soft-tissue work, posture/ergonomic assessment, and pediatric and prenatal chiropractic care. The practice handles everyday musculoskeletal complaints alongside auto-accident, sports-injury, and wellness/maintenance care. The office is located at 111 Sherlake Lane, Suite 101, Knoxville, TN 37922, and the main number is +1 865-383-7730.

How We Maintain Independence

This site is editorial — not the official website of the practice. We summarize publicly available information in plain language and link to the practice for booking and contact. We don't accept advertising and we don't take payment from the practice for coverage. For booking, billing, insurance verification, or any clinical question, contact the office directly.
